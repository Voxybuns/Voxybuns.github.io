Programming Set-ups

From June 2021 to March 2022, Nintendo has released 39 "set-ups", small demo games that showcase all kinds of game mechanics and show how to program them.
Game Builder Garage news channels were also created to promote the set-ups. Unfortunately, only the Japanese channel has shared them without interruption. The English language channels from Europe and America, on the other end, have only shared the few first set-ups, despite all 39 of them getting translated and uploaded to the Game Builder Garage online servers.
Therefore, I took it upon myself to compile all the set-ups on this webpage to provide easy access to them all, alongside short descriptions of what they do. I hope you'll find them useful when working on your own creations!
