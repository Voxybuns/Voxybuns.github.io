Programming Set-ups

Since the release of Game Builder Garage, Nintendo has been putting out weekly "set-ups", small demo games that showcase all kinds of game mechanics and how to program them. Unfortunately, while new set-ups are still being uploaded by Nintendo to this day, they are no longer being shared publicly through the News app. Therefore, I took it upon myself to compile all the set-ups on this webpage to provide easy access to all of them, alongside short descriptions of what they do.
